About Me

I am E. Sathyanesar from Chennai, currently pursuing a Bachelor of Engineering in Computer Science at Agni College of Technology. I am passionate about technology, with a strong interest in problem-solving, software development, and the practical application of core computer science concepts.

I began my web development journey with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, building responsive interfaces and projects like a Portfolio, Study Tracker, and Sorting Game. To strengthen my foundation, I earned the Responsive Web Design Certification from FreeCodeCamp gaining solid skills in modern design and mobile-first development.

I further expanded my skills by developing a Train Reservation System using Java, JavaFX, and MySQL, gaining hands-on experience in backend development and database integration.

To explore design thinking, I created UI designs using Figma, focusing on user experience, clean layouts, and visual aesthetics—strengthening my foundation in UI/UX principles.

In addition to development and design, I have completed a course in Prompt Engineering for Everyone from IBM via Cognitive Class AI, which introduced me to the fundamentals of working with large language models and crafting effective prompts—skills that are becoming increasingly valuable in modern AI-powered workflows.
